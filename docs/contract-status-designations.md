# Contract Status Designations

Contract status designations are special 1-2 character tags that appear alongside a player's contract information. There are two kinds: **persistent status** designations (RC, TO) that stay with a player across seasons and define what decisions are available to the owner throughout the life of that contract, and **decision result** designations (F) that are applied as the outcome of a single-season owner decision and can apply to any player.

---

## Active Designations

### RC – Rookie Contract
- **Type:** Persistent status
- **Characters:** 2
- **Meaning:** Player is on their initial rookie contract (non-1st round pick)
- **Available decisions:** Rookie extension only — not eligible for veteran extension or team option
- **Example:** "Anthony Richardson RC $1.2M (1 yr)"

### TO – Team Option Eligible
- **Type:** Persistent status
- **Characters:** 2
- **Meaning:** Player is a 1st round rookie with expanded contract rights
- **Available decisions:** Rookie extension or team option — owner must choose one
- **Note:** Displays as TO regardless of which decision the owner ultimately makes
- **Example:** "C.J. Stroud TO $2.5M (2 yr)"

### F – Franchise Tag
- **Type:** Decision result
- **Characters:** 1
- **Meaning:** Owner has applied the franchise tag to this player for one additional year
- **Eligible players:** Any player — franchise tag is not restricted by contract type
- **Example:** "Saquon Barkley F $12.5M (1 yr)"
